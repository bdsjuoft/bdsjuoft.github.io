AI-Powered Chatbot for Intimate/Sexual Violence Victims
Contributors
JX
Dr. Jia Xue
About

Communication in real-time messages has become pivotal for domestic violence/sexual assault survivors and agencies short of personnel and resources during the pandemic. The AI-powered Chatbot can provide 24/7 immediate responses with information about mental health screening, diagnosis, support treatment, danger evaluation, needs assessments, and resources navigation accessible via mobile phone and social media platforms (e.g., Facebook Messenger, Weibo, WeChat).
