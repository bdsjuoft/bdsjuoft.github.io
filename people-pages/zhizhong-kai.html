Kai Zhizhong is a psychotherapist and social worker in China. He is the director of Beihai Psychological Counselor Association and the director of Beihai Xinyi Social Work Service Centre. He not only has 14 years of clinical experience, but also has five yearsâ€™ experience of teaching and supervising psychotherapy. His research interests are the innovation of psychotherapy and high-tech psychometric techniques. For example, he invented a new psychotherapy called psy-chess, which is suitable for field work and social work situations. He also innovated the techniques of sand tray therapy and the recording method of sand tray therapy and applied relevant patents. He has further combined artificial intelligence with sand tray therapy to create a new tool for psychological measurement and analysis.
