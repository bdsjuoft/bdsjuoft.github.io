AIJ Laboratory Celebrates Fourth Anniversary: Remarkable Achievements and Special Awards Ceremony

January 28, 2024

The Artificial Intelligence for Justice Lab (AIJ) at the University of Toronto marked a significant milestone on January 27, 2024, as it celebrated its 4th anniversary. Founded in January 2020 by Assistant Professor Jia Xue, the lab has made remarkable strides in research and training since its inception.

‍

In the realm of research, the AIJ Lab has produced 43 research papers, firmly establishing itself as a leader in computational social science with a focus on human-centered data science. Notably, a remarkable 75% of these publications involve student contributions, showcasing the Lab’s commitment to training. In total, 45 students have benefited from the Lab’s mentorship, with 26 unique students actively contributing to publications.

‍

The AIJ Lab’s mission encompasses a wide range of topics, including Intimate Partner Violence, Sexual Assault, School Bullying, Health Information Communication, Computational Social Science, Information Communication Technology, App/Mobile Design, Data Mining, Human-Centered AI, and Human-Centered Data Science.

‍

During the 4th-anniversary event, the Lab recognized exceptional individuals with five prestigious awards, including the Publication Excellence Award (Chengda Zheng, MI), Lab Leadership Award (Qiaoru Zhang, BA), Creative Branding Visionary Award (Wenzhao Zhang, Kehan Tan, MI), Critical Thinker Award (Bolun Zhang, Doctoral student), and Data Wizard Award (Hong Shi, MI).

‍

Honoring Excellence: Special Awards Ceremony

‍

PUBLICATION EXCELLENCE AWARD

The first award, the Research Excellence Award, recognizes the individual who has actively participated in research with the most collaborations and highest citations, regardless of graduation status. This award goes to Chengda Zheng, a master's student who has collaborated on six papers with Dr. Xue, achieving a total of 660 citations. His significant contributions include crucial data collection for the Twitter project, laying a solid foundation for subsequent research. 

‍

‍

CRITICAL THINKING AWARD

The Critical Thinking Award honors Bolun Zhang, a student who has demonstrated profound understanding and unique insights into research topics. This student has shown exceptional intellectual abilities and a willingness to challenge existing knowledge boundaries. His spirit of innovation and relentless pursuit have deepened and broadened the lab's research endeavors.

‍

DATA WIZARD AWARD

The Data Wizard Award recognizes Hong Shi, a student who has excelled in data analysis, processing, and interpretation. This student's precise grasp of data details and keen insights have helped the lab avoid potential errors, ensuring research accuracy. His questioning and thorough analysis have made the lab's research work more rigorous and reliable.

‍

‍

CREATIVE BRANDING VISIONARY AWARD

The Creative Branding Visionary Award celebrates two female students, Kehan Tan and Wenzhao Zhang, who have successfully designed the lab's brand image. Their creativity and artistic talents have given the lab a unique visual language and identity, enriching the lab with a sense of beauty. Their designs, including the logo, merchandise, and the anniversary celebration cake, showcase their attention to detail and design expertise.

‍

‍

‍

LAB LEADERSHIP AWARD

The Leadership Award is given to Qiaoru Zhang, who has significantly impacted the lab's harmony and progress. With her exceptional leadership and selfless dedication, this student has profoundly influenced every member of the lab. She has significantly contributed to research projects and paper writing and has played a crucial role in daily management and member care. Her unwavering support and meticulous care have made every member feel the warmth of home.

‍

‍

Embracing the Lab Spirit: Unity and Support

In her closing remarks, Dr. Xue expressed heartfelt gratitude and set her expectations for the lab members. She envisions the lab not just as a place for academic research but as a family, a network where everyone supports and grows together. She encouraged members to continue fostering solid friendships and collaborative relationships, making the lab a robust support system in their professional journeys.

‍

"I hope the lab becomes your second home," Dr. Xue said. "Here, you are not just colleagues but brothers and sisters, supporting and growing together. This is my original intention for founding the lab and my expectation for you."

‍

‍

On this particular occasion, the AIJ Laboratory not only reflects on its past achievements but also looks forward to a bright future. Four years of dedication have built today's success, supported by every member's hard work and selfless contributions. Looking ahead, this dynamic and innovative team is poised to face new challenges and achieve more remarkable accomplishments.
