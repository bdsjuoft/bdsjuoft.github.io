About AIJ

Our Mission
Artificial Intelligence for Justice Lab (AIJ) at the University of Toronto was founded in 2018. We are dedicated to human-centered data science with missions in research and teaching.
Research
AIJ focuses its efforts to undertake new frontier research. Dedicated to human-centered data science, we tackle important questions at the leading edge of computational social science.
Explore our research across:
+ Intimate Partner Violence/Dating Violence
+ Sexual Assault/Violence, Rape
+ School Bullying
+ Health Information Communication (COVID-19)
+ Computational Social Science
+ Information Communication Technology
+ App/Mobile Design
+ Data Mining
+ Human Centered AI
+ Human Centered Data Science
View All research projects
Teaching
At a time when there is an ongoing public discussion about the responsible use of big data, the Big Data for Social Justice Lab inspires students to reflect on how big data can be used to promote social justice in innovative ways.
The Lab offers Big Data and Data Science courses IN:
+ Statistics
+ Ethics, Vulnerable Population and Information Technology
+ Research Methods
